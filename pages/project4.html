4 Degrees of Freedom Robotic Rover Arm

Project Goals

  • Design a 5DOF robotic arm
  • Support a 2kg payload
  • 1 meter reach
  • $400 Budget

Major Roadblocks

  • Motor weights increased torque requirements
    • Designed belt drive to move weight to arm base
  • 3D printed components couldn't support the torques
    • Distributed stresses along aluminum inserts

Final Outcomes

  • Designed 4DOF robotic arm with an extra DOF from the rover
  • Placed 5th at the Canadian International Rover Competition (3rd in Canada) on our first iteration
