Direct Expansion - Solar Assisted Heat Pump

Project Goals

  • Usage based on a 3 person household
  • Operational during Canadian winter climates
  • $10,000 budget

Major Roadblocks

  • Condensing unit cost over $3200 from the total budget.
    • Applied first principles to design a similar unit for $1300
  • Various selection options for choice in refrigerant, compressor, condenser, etc.
    • Created design criteria matricies to assess and weigh various factors such as environmental acceptability, safety, performance, cost, etc.

Final Outcomes

  • Built a fully functioning heat pump with all major and minor components into a transportable skid
  • Published a technical report for the ASME 2022 Congress & Expo (IMECE2022-95940)
