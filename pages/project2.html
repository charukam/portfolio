Toyota GT86 Project Car

Project Goals

  • Modifying stock car with following mods:
    • Exhaust manifold (UEL headers) to eliminate torque dip at higher RPMs
    • TVS supercharger to provide better low end torque
    • Methanol injection to increase boost levels and provide knock resistance
    • Custom coilovers for tighter handling
  • Maintaining and troubleshooting over the years
    • Oil, brake, coolant, transmission, power steering fluid changes
    • Minor car repairs

Major Roadblocks

  • Working without car lifts in tight garage space
    • Doing work with friends makes installations significantly easier

Final Outcomes

  • Stock 200hp to >300hp
  • Gained a lot of mechanical apptitude in how cars are assembled
